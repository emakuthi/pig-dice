Rules of the game

  1. The game requires two players.
  2. Any player can start at the start of the Game.
  3. Roll the dice when it's your turn to play in the game.
  4. See the score you get for each roll of the dice you make in the game.
  5. See your cumulative score for each round you play in the game. For example, if you roll the scores 2-6-3 consecutively, you should see my end score as 11.
  6. When you are satisfied with the points in that round, press hold button and the total cumulative score will be displayed.
  7. When your dice give a number 1. you will get 0 on that round and the play will be transfered to the player two.
  8. The player who reaches 100 points mark will be declared winner by an alert and game will be over.
